For my graduation project I designed a playful office to stimulate fun and reduce stress.

In childhood, play and exploration define our days. Growing older, the carefree moments fade, and are replaced by the weight of responsibilities and an endless cycle of our daily lives.

 

This new way of working is divided by tasks—focused work, collaborating, and calls—into different areas, encouraging physical movement and spontaneous interactions. The materials, shapes, colors and adaptable elements spark your curiosity and promote a playful approach to work.
