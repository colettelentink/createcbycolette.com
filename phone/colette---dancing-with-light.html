Dancing with light

The central element in the space is a large ball covered with mirror pieces sunk in rubber. While you dance with this ball, the spotlights in the ceiling follow your movements.

The mirror pieces let the light reflect throughout the space, which makes it seem as if you are dancing with the light and with yourself.

 

The five individually colored planes in the space can each illuminate a particular emotion/atmosphere during your dance.

School assignment, 2022
