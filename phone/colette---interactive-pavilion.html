Interactive pavilion

Visitors can customize the space themselves by moving the left sliding wall to create a situation that suits their own needs.

 

During a lecture, the metal walls act as a whiteboard to make notes. When you slide the wall to the left, you create a more enclosed space where you can consult quietly.
